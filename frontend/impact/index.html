impacts

Impact: Loss of Biodiversity 🦋🌍

Climate change disrupts ecosystems, leading to habitat loss, species extinction, and reduced biodiversity, affecting the balance of natural systems.

Impact: Ocean Acidification 🌊🐚

Increased CO2 levels are making oceans more acidic, threatening marine life, particularly coral reefs and shellfish.

Impact: Agricultural Decline 🌾💔

Shifting weather patterns, such as droughts and floods, harm crops, leading to food shortages and increased food prices.

Impact: Displacement of Populations 🏠🌍

The melting of glaciers and polar ice caps contributes to sea-level rise and disrupts ecosystems, particularly in cold regions.

Impact: Air Quality Deterioration 🌫️😷

Increased pollution from industrial activities, forest fires, and vehicle emissions leads to poor air quality, causing respiratory issues and health problems.

Impact: Economic Losses from Natural Disasters 💸🌪️

More frequent and severe natural disasters like hurricanes, floods, and wildfires cause substantial economic losses, affecting businesses and governments.
